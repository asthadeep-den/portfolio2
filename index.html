06 · AI Tool — Grant Diligence

Tatva — Grant Proposal Vetting Engine

An AI-powered intake-to-adjudication tool for CSR & grant diligence. Tatva ingests a proposal, a budget, and supporting materials; lets the reviewer commit to six of ten accountability dimensions; optionally authorises online verification against MCA21, CSR-1, 12A/80G and FCRA filings; scopes geography and duration; then produces a standardised proposal, costed budget, and dossier cover — plus a "what doesn't add up" pass that surfaces discrepancies at two altitudes: micro (line-item, arithmetic) and macro (mission-drift, strategic fit, capacity).

AI / LLMGrant DiligenceCSR / NGOIndia
CSR diligence breaks at two altitudes — line-item arithmetic and macro mission-drift. Tatva flags both and leaves the call to the reviewer. The judgment stays human; the desk work compresses.
